## Key Points

This book traces from the origins of money how government intervention has corrupted currency. It explains how money that naturally emerged in a free market became monopolized by the state and transformed into a hidden tax called inflation.

## Points to Consider

- You can understand why the monopoly on currency issuance is fundamentally problematic
- You can grasp the historical process of the collapse of the gold standard step by step
- It is one of the clearest books showing the essence of the problem that Bitcoin seeks to solve
