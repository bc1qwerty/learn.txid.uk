## Key Points

A dystopian novel depicting the story of Winston Smith, who lives under thought control and surveillance in the totalitarian state of 'Oceania'. It demonstrates how power manipulates language and truth through concepts like Big Brother, doublethink, and Newspeak.

## Points to Consider

- Control of currency directly correlates with control of freedom, a core thesis in the Bitcoin community
- The structure where the state rewrites history and monopolizes truth is remarkably similar to the modern financial system
- This work paradoxically illustrates why Bitcoin's immutable ledger is so important
