## Key Contents

This book explains how the monetary system has historically evolved into a layered structure. Just as banknotes are layered on top of gold and deposits are layered on top of banknotes, it interprets the structure where the Lightning Network is placed on top of Bitcoin within a historical context.

## Points to Consider

- The layered concept of money is fundamental to understanding the relationship between Bitcoin and the Lightning Network
- The role of central banks can be viewed anew from the perspective of monetary layers
- Bitcoin's potential to become the base layer of reserve currency is presented logically
