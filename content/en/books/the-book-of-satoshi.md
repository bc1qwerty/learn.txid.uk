## Key Content

This book compiles and organizes all publicly available communications from Bitcoin's creator Satoshi Nakamoto, including emails, forum posts, and code commits. Through Satoshi's own writings, you can directly examine Bitcoin's design philosophy and the rationale behind technical decisions.

## Points for Reflection

- Satoshi was not merely a technician but possessed deep understanding of economics, cryptography, and distributed systems
- Primary source materials reveal the context in which Bitcoin's initial design decisions were made
- The paradoxical and striking observation that Satoshi's disappearance contributed to Bitcoin's decentralization
