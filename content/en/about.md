## Bitsenso

I'm a Bitcoiner. I run a Bitcoin full node, do solo mining, and code as a hobby.

While studying Bitcoin, I became curious about what money is. Digging into money led me to Austrian economics, and following economics brought me to libertarian philosophy. The process of asking one question and having it lead to the next continues to this day.
