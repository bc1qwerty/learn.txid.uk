---

If you're not sure where to begin, choose the topic that interests you most from the three tracks below.

All three subjects share a common root:

- **Bitcoin** is the technological realization of [Sound Money](/ideas/sound-money/) as described by Austrian Economics
- **Austrian Economics** explains why markets work in a free society
- **Libertarianism** is the philosophy that protects individual property rights — and Bitcoin guarantees those rights through mathematics

Understand one, and the others will naturally follow.
