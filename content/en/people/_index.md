Ideas are not born in a vacuum. Behind every great idea are people who discovered it, systematized it, and shared it with the world.

The figures presented here are connected by a common thread of **individual freedom**, **sound money**, and **spontaneous order**. From 19th-century Viennese economists to 21st-century anonymous internet programmers, they each fought in their time against the expansion of state power and the debasement of money.

Understanding their ideas is not merely a matter of biographical interest, but the most reliable way to understand **why** Bitcoin and liberalism exist.
