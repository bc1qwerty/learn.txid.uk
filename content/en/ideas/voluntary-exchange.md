**Voluntary Exchange** is a transaction conducted through the free consent of both parties without coercion or fraud.

Core insights of voluntary exchange:
- Exchange benefits **both parties**. Otherwise, the transaction would not take place
- The seller values money more than the goods, while the buyer values the goods more than the money
- This difference in subjective value increases the satisfaction of both parties through exchange

Contrast with forced exchange:
- **Taxes**: Non-compliance results in punishment, so they are not voluntary
- **Regulated transactions**: When the government controls prices, voluntary exchange is distorted
- **Inflation**: Currency depreciation is a non-consensual transfer of wealth to savers

Bitcoin transactions are pure voluntary exchange. Transactions only occur when both parties sign with their private keys, and no third party can force or prevent the transaction.
