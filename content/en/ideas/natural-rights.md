**Natural Rights** are inherent rights that originate from human existence itself, not granted by the state or laws.

The three natural rights systematized by John Locke:
- **Life**: The right to protect one's own survival
- **Liberty**: The right to act freely as long as one does not infringe on the rights of others
- **Property**: The right to own what one has acquired through one's own labor

Natural Rights vs Legal Rights:
- Natural rights are discovered, not created
- The state does not grant natural rights but has a duty to protect them
- Laws that contradict natural rights are unjust laws

Bitcoin protects the natural right of property through cryptography. In that it allows one to mathematically protect one's property without the protection of laws or the state, it can be said to be a technological realization of natural rights.
