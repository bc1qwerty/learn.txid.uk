**Anarcho-Capitalism** is a philosophy that advocates for the complete abolition of the state, with all services including law, security, and national defense entrusted to free market competition.

The core logic of this philosophy, systematized by Murray Rothbard:
1. The state is the only institution with a legitimate monopoly on violence
2. Monopolies always lead to inefficiency and abuse
3. Therefore, the functions of the state could be better performed through competitive markets

Specifically:
- **Security**: Private security companies provide services competitively
- **Law**: Private arbitration firms resolve disputes
- **Currency**: Market-chosen currency replaces the state's monopoly currency

Bitcoin can be viewed as a realization of anarcho-capitalism in the monetary sphere. It operates without state permission and has successfully implemented a currency system that no one can control.
